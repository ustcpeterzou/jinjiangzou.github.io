I am a fourth year undergraduate student in the School of Gifted Young at the University of Science and Technology of China.

I am majoring in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics with a focus on Probability Theory and Mathematical Statistics. My research interests include high-dimensional statistics, statistical machine learning, and stochastic processes.

I am currently working on high-dimensional dynamic pricing problems under the supervision of Professor Zheng. Previously, I worked on Bayesian deep learning methods with Professor Wang.
