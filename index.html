About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Science and Technology. My research focuses on developing novel algorithms and theoretical foundations for machine learning systems.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University in 2020, where I was advised by Prof. Jane Smith. My dissertation focused on "Scalable Optimization Methods for Deep Learning."

Prior to my current position, I was a postdoctoral researcher at MIT, working on reinforcement learning and robotics applications.
